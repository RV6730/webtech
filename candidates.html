Election Rules & Guidelines

All students participating in the Student Council elections must carefully read and understand the following rules:

  • Only currently enrolled students with valid ID cards are eligible to vote
  • Each student can vote only once
  • Voting is confidential - no one will have access to your individual choices
  • Campaigning within 50 meters of polling stations is prohibited
  • Voting period: October 15-17, 2025 (9:00 AM to 5:00 PM daily)
